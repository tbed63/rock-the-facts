How to Play

  1. Choose one factor from the top row and one factor from the bottom row.
  2. Multiply the two factors in your head. The product stays hidden.
  3. Click the matching product square to claim it.
  4. After the first turn, each player must move exactly one factor before claiming a square.
  5. If you click the wrong product, you lose your turn.
  6. First player to get 4 tokens in a row wins.
