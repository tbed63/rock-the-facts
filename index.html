How to Play

  1. Choose one factor from the top row and one from the bottom row.
  2. Multiply them in your head — the product stays hidden.
  3. Click the matching product square on the board to claim it.
  4. After the first turn, move exactly one factor before claiming a square.
  5. Click the wrong product and you lose your turn.
  6. First player to get 4 tokens in a row wins!
