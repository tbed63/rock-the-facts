How to Play

Pick one factor from each side. Mentally multiply them. Click the product square you think is correct. If you click the wrong product, you lose your turn. After the first turn, each player may change only one factor. First player to get 4 tokens in a row wins. Same factor is allowed, like 7 × 7.
